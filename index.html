Welcome to Shaheed Salahuddin Cantonment Internet Service

We are providing high-quality internet services to military personnel in Bangladesh. We are proud to be a non-profit enterprise, dedicated to meeting the communication needs of our soldiers serving inside the cantonments. Our mission is to provide fast, reliable and affordable internet services that help military personnel stay connected with their families, access important information and entertainment, and stay connected with the world

Abut us

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to providing the highest level of service and support to our customers. We use the latest technologies and equipment to deliver fast and reliable internet services that meet the needs of our users. Our dedicated support team is available 24/7 to provide assistance and resolve any issues that may arise. As a non-profit enterprise, our focus is solely on providing high-quality internet services to military personnel. We do not engage in commercial activities or profit-making ventures, and all revenues generated are reinvested in the company to improve our services and support our mission.

We are committed to building strong relationships with our customers and providing a personalized experience. We listen to our customers' feedback and strive to improve our services and processes to meet their changing needs. Our goal is to be the internet service provider of choice for military personnel in Bangladesh, and we are working hard every day to achieve this goal. Thank you for choosing our ISP. We are honored to serve you and are committed to providing you with the best possible internet experience. If you have any questions or feedback, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are here to help you stay connected with your loved ones and the world.

Privacy policy



The Shaheed Salahuddin Cantonment Internet Service ("SSCIS") Privacy Policy was last updated on April 17, 2023. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully and contact us at info@sscisp.com if you have any questions. This privacy policy sets out how SSCIS collects, uses, discloses, stores and protects any information that you provide when you subscribe to use our internet service(s). SSCIS is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and we do not share any of your information with any other third parties except where required by law. We may ask you to provide certain information depending on the contents and services you use so that we can identify you and provide you appropriate service. Collection and Use of Personal Information What personal information we collect and how we use it are listed here: When you subscribe to SSCIS services, you may have to provide us with some of your personal information, including your name, address, email address, phone number, date of birth, National Identity Number, and your passport size photograph. We may collect your location information and device identity to identify you concisely to provide our services. When you access our internet services, we provide an authentication number along with your user ID and other information that is saved to your device to let you use SSCIS securely for the entire session, and during logout, we erase all data that we provided during login. During your monthly bill payment, our payment gateways require your debit/credit card information or your mobile banking information to successfully complete online bill payment. How we use your personal information We may use your personal information for the following purposes with your consent: We may use your personal information to verify your identity, verify your bill payments and provide you specific services that you bought from us. We may use your email, phone, device identity, and other information to periodically notify you about service updates, changes, purchases, billings, dues, payments, and your other activities through notifications, emails, messages, phone calls, and other communicative ways that you allowed by subscriptions. Collection and Use of Non-Personal Information We may collect some non-personal information and store it to provide you standard quality services. Non-personal information that we collect is listed here: We may collect, use, and store data related to your internet usage such as data usage, browsing history, and websites visited. We may collect your location information to keep logs of your signing activities and secure your signing in by making alerts to you. Controlling Your Personal Information You may choose to restrict the collection or use of your personal information in the following ways: Whenever you are asked to fill in a form on the website or application, look for the fields properly that you want or do not want the information to be used by our services. If you have previously agreed to us using your personal information for service purposes, you may change your mind at any time by writing to us. Use of Third-Party Library or SDKs We do not integrate any third-party libraries or SDKs into SSCIS internet services. Protection of Your Information SSCIS is committed to ensuring the security of your data that you provided. In order to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we store your data in our server which complies all standard security parameters. Your Privacy Rights We do not share, sell, disclose, distribute, or lease any of your personal information to any other third parties unless we have your consent and permission or required by law to do so. You may request any time to correct, erase or permanently remove all of your information that you provided us and if you do so we are obliged to process your request. You can take any actions with legal law against us if we violate any part of this privacy policy
